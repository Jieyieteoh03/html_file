Environmentally Friendly and Hypoallergenic.

What is HYPO?

HYPO is the perfect solution to making life with allergies and other skin sensitivities more comfortable for men, women, and children. These products include latex/spandex-free and chemical-free 100% clothing. HYPO eliminates the use of bleaching compounds, color dyes, and textile resins that can pose a threat to sensitive skin. The cotton used is 100% organically-grown and the fabric is highly absorbent and breathable.

Why choose HYPO?

HYPO means quality and responsibility. From sowing to sewing, we take pride and pleasure in cotton that's certifiably made from eco-friendly and chemical-free materials. We do not compromise our principles. By choosing HYPO, you are making a statement that living life with allergies can be better while helping to protect our environment.

How Eco-Friendly is HYPO?

Our materials and processes remain environmentally friendly and safe for people with skin allergies and sensitive skin.
Here's how:

100% Organic Cotton Fabric

We buy our yarn from GOTS-certified organic cotton farms. In these areas, the water supply is not contaminated by pesticides or any other harmful chemicals or toxins.

100% Cotton Thread

Clothing manufacturers regularly use microplastic materials like polyester sewing thread to keep the garments affordable and easy to produce. However, these can be detrimental to people with skin conditions and sensitive skin.

Hypoallergenic Coco Buttons

Completely natural in origin, these coco buttons are applied to our casual clothes to provide a total allergy-free experience. They're smooth, durable, and eco-friendly.

100% Compostable Packaging

This new compostable packaging features a Kraft paper top layer and an inner lining crafted from cornstarch-derived PLA material to significantly lessen the environmental impact after use.
